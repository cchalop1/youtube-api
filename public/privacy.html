Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how our Custom GPT processes data when summarizing YouTube videos. We are committed to user privacy and transparency. This project is open-source, and we do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

2. Data Processing

  • The GPT processes YouTube video transcripts to generate summaries.
  • No user data, video history, or metadata is collected, stored, or shared.
  • All processing occurs in real-time without data retention.

3. Open-Source Commitment

  • The codebase is fully open-source, allowing users to verify our privacy practices.
  • Users can review, modify, and deploy the solution independently.

4. User Control

  • Users retain full control over their interactions with the model.
  • No login, authentication, or tracking mechanisms are used.

5. Security & Compliance

  • We do not process or store any sensitive or personally identifiable information.
  • The tool operates in a stateless manner, ensuring no persistent data is retained.

6. Changes to This Policy

As an open-source project, updates may be made to improve functionality, but privacy principles will remain unchanged. Any modifications will be transparent and documented in the repository.

7. Contact & Transparency

For any questions regarding privacy or the open-source project, users are encouraged to engage with the repository or reach out through the provided communication channels.

By using this tool, users acknowledge and agree to its privacy-friendly nature.
